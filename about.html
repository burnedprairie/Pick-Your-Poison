Shipping Policy

All items are made to order.

Kindly allow 4-6 weeks for production time. Orders submitted after 2PM will commence processing the subsequent business day.

All U.S. orders are charged a flat rate shipping cost of $10. All international orders are charged a flat rate shipping cost of $30.

Per USPS estimates, domestic deliveries typically take 2-8 business days, while international shipping may require more time.

Should a delivery issue arise due to an address entry error, Pick Your Poison is not liable for any extra fees incurred for rerouting or reshipment.

Customers are responsible for all taxes and duties imposed by their country of residence or the destination of the shipped package.

Returns

Due to our items being made-to-order, there is always an exclusive quantity. Because of this, all sales are final *no refunds or exchanges*.

For incorrect, defective, or damaged items, please contact us immediately upon receiving your order, so we can evaluate the issue and make it right.

For any questions regarding returns, please reach us at poisonprincessgirl@gmail.com.
